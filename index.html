Welcome to David Norman Landscape Gardens

David Norman Landscapes offers quality and reliable landscape gardening services.

We are a Landscape Gardening company, established in 1987, that works to a high standard providing the customer with a friendly, professional, conscientious service. We specialise in projects for domestic customers and private companies, we also do a lot of work for local authorities in South London, Kent and Surrey.

Our high standards keep us in work throughout the year on a recommendation basis as customer satisfaction is our priority.

What We Offer

  • Hard Landscaping
  • Ponds & Water Features
  • Garden Lighting
  • Walls & Patios
  • Decking & Bespoke Joinery
  • Soft Landscaping
  • Boundary Fences & Feature Screens
  • Pergodas & Summer Houses
  • Quality Lawns & Artificial Grass
  • Garden Plant Supply

  • Tree Surgery & Pruning
  • ... And More!
